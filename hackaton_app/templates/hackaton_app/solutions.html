Healthcare problems: Inefficienty and Waste Causes

Implementing electronic document management (EDM) systems and digitizing paperwork can reduce bureaucracy and increase efficiency. Electronic health record (EHR) management systems can improve information sharing across departments and reduce waiting times.

The use of IoT (Internet of Things) to monitor medical equipment and
hospital resources in real time can reduce waste and optimize resource use.

Excess of bureaucracy

Adoption of telemedicine solutions can reduce wait times and improve access to care, especially in rural areas. Patients can receive consultations and monitoring remotely, reducing the need for in-person visits, thereby reducing long and tedious queues.

Fraud Control and Prevention Systems:

The implementation of advanced control systems and data analytics is a key strategy for identifying and preventing fraud and abuse in the health care system. Such systems can contribute significantly to improving the efficiency and transparency of healthcare operations, reducing costs and increasing the quality of services provided to patients.
